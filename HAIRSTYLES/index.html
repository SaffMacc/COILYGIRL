Cornrows

This adorable hairstyle features cornrows in the front which retain moisture into the hair promoting growth. Only the back of the hair will require daily maintenance.

Locs

Locs keeps the moisture into the hair allowing length retention. It also requires little maintenance so this reduces hair breakage that occurs from combing the hair.

Space Buns With Curls At The Front

The braided buns at the back retain moisture into the hair whilst the springy curls at the front gives the hairstyle a gorgeous finish.

Curly Bun Mowhawk

This trendy hairstyle protects your curls as they are wrapped up allowing them to stay moisturised. It is just as beneficial for curly hair as it looks!

Twists

This two strand twist is a cute and protective style for girls with natural curls. A versatile look, these twists can be worn down,or tied up in a bun, ponytail or pigtails.

Braids On The Side With Beads

How cute is this side braided style! This braided hairstyle keeps hair in place whilst the colourful beads are the perfect way to involve her sense of style in the process.

Kiddie French Braids
